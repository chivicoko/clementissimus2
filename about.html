A LITTLE HISTORY THIS CLEMENTISIMUS VOICE

Clementisimus Voice of The Voiceless is a group of singers formed in the year 2020 by Bro Clement Chibuikem Okafor, a Seminarian of the Holy Mother Church with the assistance of a few individuals. The group is comprised of young singers, most of whom are still sudents in Nigeria, while some are young workers and job sickers both within and beyond Nigeria.

Clementisimus voice of The Voiceless is dedicated to helping the Christendom grow more effectively, and also to touch lives through and through other means available to us.

Clementisimus Voice of The Voiceless has done a lot of refreshing song renditions that soothes the soul of man. You will see some of these posts in our Blog and Services pages.

There two priest of the Holy Mother Church who assist in this spiritual journey of ours. They help direct us in many ways. These priests, Rev. fr. Paulinus Okafor and Rev. Fr. Jude Ozumba help us immensely in our renditions in a way That helps us meet the spiritual desires of the people of God.

BRO CLEMENT CHIBUIKEM OKAFOR

Bro Clement Chibuikem Okafor is a seminarian of the Holy Mother Church. He is Philosophy student at Seat of wisdom Seminary, Owerri which is affiliated to the university of Calabar, Nigeria.

Clement Chibuikem Okafor hails from Urunnevo village, Enugwu-ukwu in Njikoka Local Government Area, Anambra State, Nigeria. He is a very devout seminarian and a very industrious individual. He has a very large vision for the people of God, and he is so passionate about achieving that vision.

REV. FR. JUDE OZUMBA

Rev. Fr. Jude Ozumba is a priest of the Holy Mother Church. He has served as a priest in different parishes and mission schools. And is presently serving at St.Anthony's Catholic Church, Obodo-Obodo.

Rev. Fr. Jude Ozumba hails from Urunnevo village, Enugwu-ukwu in Njikoka Local Government Area, Anambra State, Nigeria. He is a very devout priest and a youth-friendly individual with a very large heart.

REV. FR. PAULINUS OKAFOR

Rev. Fr. Paulinus Okafor is a priest of the Holy Mother Church. He has served as a priest in different parishes and mission schools. And is presently serving at St.Anthony's Catholic Church, Obodo-Obodo.

Rev. Fr. Paulinus Okafor hails from Uru-uru village, Ichida in Anaocha Local Government Area, Anambra State, Nigeria. He is a very devout priest and a youth-friendly individual with a very large heart.
